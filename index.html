About Us

Our Story

Founded on a passion for farming and community upliftment, Nkadimeng Agri has been operating informally since 2021 and officially registered as a company in 2024. Rooted in Lynnville, Emalahleni, we are committed to producing high-quality, organic chicken for our local community.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of organic poultry products in Mpumalanga and beyond, empowering communities through food security and sustainable farming practices.

Our Mission

To produce and supply premium-quality organic chickens that meet the nutritional needs of our community, promote sustainable and ethical farming methods, and foster strong relationships with local customers.
