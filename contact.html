Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us at 060 611 0490, sending us an email at Nkadimeng.agri@gmail.com, or by visiting our farm directly during business hours.

Do you deliver?

We currently offer delivery within the Emalahleni area for larger orders. For smaller purchases, we encourage customers to visit our farm to ensure maximum freshness.

What makes your chickens organic?

Our chickens are raised without antibiotics, hormones, or harmful chemicals. They have access to outdoor spaces, are fed organic feed, and are raised following sustainable farming practices.

Can I visit your farm?

Yes! We welcome visitors to our farm during business hours. It's a great opportunity to see our sustainable farming practices firsthand and purchase fresh products directly.

Do you offer wholesale prices?

Yes, we offer competitive wholesale pricing for restaurants, retailers, and other businesses. Please contact us directly to discuss your needs and pricing options.
