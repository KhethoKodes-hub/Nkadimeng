Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between live and freshly slaughtered chickens?

Live chickens are sold as is for those who prefer to process their own poultry. Freshly slaughtered chickens are humanely processed, cleaned, and prepared for immediate cooking or freezing.

How do I store the chickens?

Freshly slaughtered chickens should be refrigerated and consumed within 2-3 days, or frozen for up to 6 months. Live chickens should be processed within 24 hours of purchase.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within the Emalahleni area for orders above a certain quantity. Delivery fees may apply. Contact us for specific delivery options and pricing.

Can I visit the farm before purchasing?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors to our farm during business hours. It's a great opportunity to see our sustainable farming practices firsthand.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, EFT, and mobile payments. Payment is required at the time of order confirmation for scheduled pickups or deliveries.
