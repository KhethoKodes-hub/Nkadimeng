About Nkadimeng Agri

Learn about our journey, mission, and commitment to sustainable farming

Our Story

Rooted in Community, Growing with Purpose

Nkadimeng Agri was born from a passion for farming and a deep commitment to community upliftment. What began as an informal operation in 2021 has grown into a proudly registered South African agricultural enterprise, officially established in 2024.

Based in the heart of Lynnville, Emalahleni, we recognized the pressing need for reliable, high-quality food sources in our community. With food security challenges affecting many households, we positioned ourselves not just as suppliers, but as active contributors to building sustainable local food systems.

Our journey reflects our values: starting small, thinking locally, and always prioritizing the well-being of our community and environment. Every chicken we raise represents our commitment to ethical practices, quality products, and the people we serve.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To produce and supply premium-quality organic chickens that meet the nutritional needs of our community while promoting sustainable, ethical farming methods that protect the environment and support animal welfare.

We foster strong relationships with local customers by delivering fresh, healthy, and affordable products directly from our farm to their tables.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of organic poultry products in Mpumalanga and beyond, empowering communities through food security and sustainable farming practices.

We envision a future where every household has access to healthy, locally-produced food that supports both personal wellness and community economic growth.

Our Values

Sustainability

We champion farming methods that protect natural resources for future generations, ensuring our practices leave the land healthier than we found it.

Community

We exist to serve and uplift the people of Emalahleni and surrounding areas, building relationships that extend beyond business transactions.

Quality

Our commitment is to consistently provide products that are fresh, healthy, and ethically sourced, never compromising on standards.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and a customer-first attitude in all our interactions and business decisions.
